About Me

Jacqueline Galdamez is currently a waiter at upscale restaurant Burton's Grill & Bar which serves American food. Although she's been in the restaurant industry for four years she decided to make a change in careers by continuing her education. After saving money and doing a bunch of research she came across the web developement program provided by George Washington University. Having a passion for learning and improving has been her motivation to study Web Developement in which there is plenty of room for growth. What thrives her to want more in life would always be her family. Growing up with hispanic traditions she never lost touch of the culture and her family help her realize that opportunities shouldn't be taken for granted here in the US. She was born in Los Angeles, California, with spanish being her first language, but moved to Virginia at a young age. Being raised in the Alexandria area she's had quite a bit of experiences such as photography, cosmetology, and soccer.
